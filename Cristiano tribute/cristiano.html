CRISTIANO RONALDO PORTUGUESE PLAYER AND THE GOAT

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Serie A club Juventus and captains the Portugal national team.
Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time,[note 3]
Ronaldo has five Ballons d'Or[note 4]
